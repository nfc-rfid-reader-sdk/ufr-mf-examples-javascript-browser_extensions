This demo serves as an uFR Simple implementation in browser along with extension and uFR shell.


For port open with your reader you have option of choosing ReaderOpen() or ReaderOpenEx(). For ReaderOpenEx, all you need to do is check the 'Advanced options' checkbox and provide suitable arguments such as reader type, port name/interface etc. Upon clicking Reader Open, whether you selected Advanced options or default ones, it will connect to your reader on success, and display reader type and serial number in given text boxes.
Get Card Info will call GetCardIdEx() function and display card information such as card type, card UID (card serial field) and UID size.
Rest of functions in this example and their parameters are explained in following document:

Mifare Examples

https://www.d-logic.net/code/nfc-rfid-reader-sdk/ufr-doc.git
